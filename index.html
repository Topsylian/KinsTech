Retreat

licon Spa is a tranquil sanctuary for ultimate relaxation offering a range of indulgent treatments and therapies - tucked away in a corner of our beautiful gardens, it's available for our guests to enjoy for a blissful amount of relaxation and me time during an overnight stay at licon Hotel.

Awaken

A secret hideaway enveloped by gorgeous gardens, the spa is designed to help you recharge, revive and feel heavenly from head-to-toe. We believe in the power of nature which is why you'll find natural and botanical elements from the garden all around the spa… indoor plants, tables crafted from tree trunks and lavender placed on treatment beds.
